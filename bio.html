Biography

Yipei Niu received his B.Engr. degree in School of Computer and Information Engineering, Henan University, China. He is currently a Ph.D. student in School of Computer Science and Technology, Huazhong University of Science and Technology, China. His research interests include cloud computing, FPGA for acceleration, and container networks. Detailed information can be found in his CV.
